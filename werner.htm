Tie Creek


These pictures are from my recent trip to Tie Creek Petroform on October 12, 2014. Everything appears undisturbed. However, I noticed a wooden offering bowl, at the site. I appreciate the site being used for healing purposes, but I hope to not see any more additions. Bannock Point Petroforms area is far over used and most of the ancient petroforms have been disturbed. I would not like to see that happen to the Tie Creek site.
