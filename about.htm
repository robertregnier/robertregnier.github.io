ABOUT US

 

 

Joanne Francoeur grew up on Vancouver Island and is now living in White Rock, British Columbia.  She comes from a creative family and has enjoyed working mainly with water colours and acrylics.  Joanne enjoys painting murals, canvases and furniture as well as boxes.  She teaches art to children and has been doing so since 1985 as a Preschool Teacher, Mom and in elementary schools.   Recently, Joanne has created original murals, boarders and details for those clients to wish to add a personal touch to their home.
